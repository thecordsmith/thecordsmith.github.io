About The Cordsmith

Our Story

The Cordsmith is a passion project born from a love for crafting and creativity. We specialize in handwoven keychains, monkey fists, and more, each piece meticulously crafted to blend traditional knotting techniques with modern design.

Meet Pranav

Hi, I'm Pranav, a high school sophomore from Coppell, Texas. As a coder and crafter, I founded The Cordsmith to share my unique designs. When I'm not weaving cords, I'm coding new features for this site or discovering movies on Netflix!
