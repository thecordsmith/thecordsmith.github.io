About The CordSmith

Hi, I'm Pranav Prabhu, a sophomore at Coppell High School in Coppell, Texas. I started The CordSmith to share my passion for creating high-quality, handwoven paracord products. Each keychain, monkey fist keychain, bracelet, and lanyard is made with precision and care, using premium materials to ensure durability and style.

My goal is to create unique, functional accessories that reflect individuality and craftsmanship. Whether you're looking for a sturdy keychain or a stylish bracelet, every piece is designed to stand out. Thanks for supporting my small business!
